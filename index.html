Get To Know Us

About Us

FANBAT Engineering Company Limited (FECL) was established as an Engineering service company dedicated to providing high quality and cost-effective Mechanical & Electrical Engineering services to customers. With its headquarters in Lagos and site offices opened on temporary basis for effective job execution in various locations. FECL is well positioned to serve its clients’ needs across the country while providing excellent support services by leveraging on our deep industry experience and expertise.

Our Vision

FECL envisions to be a world class infrastructure service provider in the Mechanical & Electrical Engineering Services industry.

Our Mission

FECL mission is to be the company of choice in M&E Engineering Services procurement & construction by deploying best practice methodologies to achieve customer satisfaction

Our Core Values

We act with integrity, deploy cost efficient strategies that align with our project management methodology and are focused on our customer and partner’s satisfaction. We work together as a team to deliver consistent and exceptional services bearing in mind. to constantly uphold the highest professional standards.

Client Liaison

One of FECL’s key strengths lie in the ability to ensure continuous and consistent feedback is provided to clients to keep them abreast of the status of each project. This also allows us to remain flexible and open to possible changes in the course of executing a project/ task.

Our Services

Electrical Engineering

We deal with all forms of electrical and electronic works such as Transformer and RMU Installation, Installation of Electrical Piping, Wiring and Light Fitting.

Telecommunications

We also render services in the Telecommunications sectors, some of our services include Installation and Mounting of Poles at Telecommunication Site.

Power Engineering

Leveraging on our industry experience we render a wide range of power generation & transmission services such as Transmission & Distribution of Overhead Network.

Haulage

We have a Haulage unit that ensures timely delivery on our services, some of our Haulage Services include Transportation of heavy machineries, Lifting and Positioning of heavy machineries, Long and Short distance route transportation.

Mechanical Engineering

We provide a wide range of Mechanical Engineering services some of which include Design & Installation of HVAC Systems, Installation of Plumbing System, Installation of Water Treatment & Sewage Treatment, Construction of Borehole.

Civil Engineering

At FECL we render a range of Civil Engineering services some of which include Construction of shelter, generator, tank, AVR and transformer plinths, Construction of Tower base, buildings and infrastructure.
